## Welcome to PayAI

PayAI is building the tools and products for a future of agentic commerce, where AI Agents can coordinate and transact with one another seamlessly. 

Our open-source technologies empower developers to create and monetize AI agents and services.

This documentation provides everything you need to get started with our products.
